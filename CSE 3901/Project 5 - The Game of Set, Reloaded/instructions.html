The Game of Set is a card game.

The deck consists of 81 cards of 4 features: number, color, shape, and shading. The game starts by dealing 12 cards.

Number: 1, 2, 3

Color: Red, Purple, Green

Shape: Diamond Squiggly, Oval

Shading: Solid, Striped, Open

The rules are simple: you must find 3 cards on the table that form a set: each individual characteristic is either the exact same, or all different.

In other words, you must avoid having 2 cards showing 1 version of the feature and the remaining card showing a different version.

For example, if each card was green and each shape is a circle, then it follows the quantities are 1,2,3 and the shade is empty,half-full, and full in order for it to be a set.

After finding a set, the cards you found will be removed and 3 more cards will be dealt to keep 12 cards on the table.

If a set does not exists on the table, 3 more cards will be dealt again (15 cards on the table).

The game is over when no sets are left to be found.
